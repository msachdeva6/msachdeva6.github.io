Our Story

Led by Mridul Sachdeva, Desi Zaika Express brings over a decade of culinary passion from the heart of India to the streets of Scarborough. We specialize in bringing the nostalgic, vibrant flavors of Indian "Galis" to Canada—where every spice blend is handcrafted and every bite tells a story.
